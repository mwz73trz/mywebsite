About Me

I am a 22 year Air Force veteran that loves to troubleshoot and solve problems. I began my Air Force career as a crew chief at Dover AFB, DE, working on C-5 Galaxy aircraft. I then switch gears to teaching C-130 Hercules maintenance at Wichita Falls, TX followed by an assignment at Charleston AFB, SC, working on C-17 Globe Master aircraft. While stationed at Charleston I began a bachelors degree in Information Technology at American Military University learning software programming in C#, OOP, SQL, and a little Java. From Charleston I shipped off to Andersen AFB, Guam, where I served as a production superintendent. While at Guam I started my masters degree in IT/Enterprise Software Development. Writing and researching software development life cycle topics and program management. My final station, before retirement took me to where I am now, Scott AFB, IL, where I was a logistics readiness controller and finished my masters degree. Just recently I completed the 14 week bootcamp at Code Platoon, learning Python, JavaScript, Django, React, PostgreSQL, UnitTest, Jest, Git, and completed the Agile SAFe 5 certification.

Besides enjoying to code and solve problems, I spend time with my wife, two children, and two dogs. My daughter keeps us busy with track and cross country, while my son is in soccer, baseball, basketball, and track. I love sports to include baseball, football, hockey, and NASCAR.
